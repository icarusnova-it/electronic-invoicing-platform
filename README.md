# Electronic Invoicing Platform

Enterprise-grade electronic invoicing platform designed to comply with
tax authority regulations while remaining scalable and extensible.

## Capabilities
- Electronic invoice issuance and reception
- Tax authority integration (SRI, SUNAT, DIAN, etc.)
- Validation and authorization workflows
- Digital signatures and XML management

## Architecture Focus
- Regulatory compliance by design
- High availability
- Secure document handling
- Auditability and traceability

## Structure
- /docs → Functional and regulatory documentation
- /diagrams → System and integration diagrams
- /adr → Architectural decisions
- /examples → Sample flows and integrations

## Audience
Enterprises, fintechs, ERP integrators, and government-compliant platforms.
